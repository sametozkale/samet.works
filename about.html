About me

Over 9+ years, I've taken products from zero to thousands of users and turned ideas into award-winning, funded ventures. I don't just design; I build, ship, and iterate. I scaled Producter to 3,500+ users across 18 time zones, then co-founded Mues AI as Design Lead, building agentic AI cursor technology that transforms how people interact with software.

My edge is the balance of vision and execution: I've shipped award-winners and learned from failures, raised capital and bootstrapped. If you need someone who bridges design, product strategy, and AI, thinking deeply and executing quickly, let's talk.
