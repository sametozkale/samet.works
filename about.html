About me

Over the past 9+ years, I've built products from zero to thousands of users, led cross-functional teams across multiple time zones, and transformed ideas into award-winning products. My approach combines strategic thinking with hands-on execution—I don't just design, I build, ship, and iterate.

Leadership, for me, means creating clarity in ambiguity. I've learned that the best products emerge when teams feel empowered to make decisions, when information flows transparently, and when everyone understands the "why" behind what we're building.

This philosophy helped me scale Producter to 3,500+ users across 18 time zones, win industry awards, and raise funding—all while maintaining a culture where people genuinely enjoy working together.

Currently exploring new opportunities, previously built something new at Mues AI as Co-founder, AI & Design Lead, working on agentic AI cursor technology that transforms user interactions with software products.

What sets my work apart is the balance between vision and execution. I've shipped products that won awards, but I've also learned from failures. I've raised capital, but I've also bootstrapped. This range of experience means I understand every part of the product journey, from initial concept to scaling challenges.

If you're looking for someone who can bridge design, product strategy, and AI (someone who thinks deeply but executes quickly), I'd love to explore how we can work together. You can find me sharing thoughts on LinkedIn and X / Twitter
